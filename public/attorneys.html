Founder & Managing Partner

Adv. Laxmikant Patne

B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) · LL.M.

Adv. Laxmikant Patne founded Patne Law Associates in 1995 and leads the firm's litigation practice. He has extensive experience across service matters, constitutional law, civil disputes, property matters, and High Court litigation, and has appeared in significant matters before the Supreme Court of India.

His strategic approach and courtroom expertise have contributed materially to the firm's reputation. He insists on preparation that begins long before a hearing — and on advocacy that is restrained, doctrinal, and clear.

Senior Partner

Dr. Neerja Patne

B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) · LL.M. · Ph.D. (Law)

Dr. Neerja Patne combines academic excellence with practical legal expertise. Her doctoral scholarship and her grounding in legal research strengthen the firm's ability to provide comprehensive solutions across diverse practice areas — from family and consumer matters to constitutional advocacy.

She leads much of the firm's family-law practice with particular emphasis on client privacy, considered counsel, and — wherever the dispute permits — durable resolution through mediation.

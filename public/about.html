The Firm

Counsel, in the older sense of the word.

Patne Law Associates was founded on the conviction that great advocacy begins with patient counsel — and that the firm a client retains says as much as the case they bring.

Our Story

Thirty years of considered practice.

Adv. Laxmikant PatneFounding Partner

The firm opened its doors in Indore in 1995 with a single chamber, a small library, and a determination to take only matters that we could prepare for properly. Three decades later, that principle remains unchanged — even as the practice has grown to appear before the Supreme Court of India, multiple High Courts, and tribunals across Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Delhi, and Aurangabad.

Our reputation has been built quietly: through the quality of our pleadings, the discretion of our representation, and the relationships we have kept with clients across generations. We do not advertise outcomes. We let our work, and our standing at the bar, speak for itself.

Today the firm covers nineteen distinct disciplines — from constitutional matters and service law to GST adjudication, revenue practice, and family work — operating as a single, integrated chambers under shared standards of preparation and conduct.

Milestones

Three decades, briefly told.

1995

The chambers are founded.

Adv. Laxmikant Patne establishes the practice with a focus on civil litigation and constitutional matters before the High Court.

2003

First Supreme Court appearance.

The firm argues its inaugural special leave petition before the apex court, beginning a continuing practice at India's highest forum.

2009

Dr. Neerja Patne joins as Partner.

The firm expands into family law, succession, and a refined civil practice — anchored by Dr. Patne's doctoral scholarship.

2015

Multi-state practice consolidated.

Standing appearances established across the High Courts of Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Delhi, and Aurangabad, alongside specialist tribunals.

2018

Dedicated GST and tax bench.

A specialist team is formed to handle the indirect-tax regime introduced in 2017, advising listed and unlisted enterprises.

2025

Nineteen disciplines, one standard.

The firm marks its thirtieth year with an integrated practice across nineteen disciplines and thousands of clients represented.

What We Hold To

The standards that govern the work.

N° 01

Preparation over performance.

A case is built in the months before the hearing. We over-prepare, then argue with restraint. Drama belongs in fiction.

N° 02

Counsel, not commerce.

We accept matters we believe we can advance honestly. Where another forum or counsel would serve the client better, we say so.

N° 03

Discretion as default.

The names of our clients, the contents of their files, and the strategies we pursue stay within chambers. Without exception.

N° 04

Doctrinal fidelity.

We argue from primary sources and current precedent. We do not bend the law to a brief; we shape the brief to the law.

N° 05

Plain language.

Our pleadings are clear, our advice is plain, and our fees are stated in advance. Obscurity is not a virtue.

N° 06

The long view.

We measure success in client relationships sustained across decades — and in the precedent our work leaves on the record.
